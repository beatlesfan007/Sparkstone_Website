Sparkstone Trading Challenge
sponsored by



The Sparkstone Trading Challenge provides a chance for Cornell students to learn about algorithmic trading and gain real-world experience building strategies on our proprietary analytics platform!

This past semester was the first Trading Challenge with $400 in total prizes. Representatives from our sponsors for the competition, Optiver came to hand out the prizes with us as well as celebrate the winning teams with a dinner.

The challenge will take place from April 4 until May 4. Registration is free, and will remain open until March 25th. You can register as a one person team, pick a partner, or let our system match you with someone to complement your skills. For example, if you have particular strengths in finance, we will do our best to match you with someone with a coding background to help implement your strategy ideas! Please review our Terms and Conditions below before registering.

Given the success of this trading challenge, come this fall 2016, we will be hosting this competition again. If you missed out before, now is your chance to try your hand at algorithmic trading. For those who competed this year, we hope you will join us again!

Update: Registration is closed as of March 26th. Thanks to everyone who applied.
